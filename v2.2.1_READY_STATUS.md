## üìû Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Questions

**Q: "How accurate is this?"**  
A: Statistical accuracy improves with more games. 50+ games recommended for reliable results.

**Q: "What does CRITICAL mean?"**  
A: Win rate below 30% - extremely exploitable weakness.

**Q: "Should I always play the weakest opening?"**  
A: Yes! Play it consistently if you prepare well. It's a proven weakness.

**Q: "Can I use this for other purposes?"**  
A: Yes! Analyze your own games to find YOUR weaknesses and improve.

### Troubleshooting

**Issue**: No games found  
**Solution**: Check username spelling, verify account is public, try another user

**Issue**: Unexpected results  
**Solution**: More games = better data. Analyze 50+ games minimum.

**Issue**: Import errors  
**Solution**: Verify chess_analyzer/exploit.py exists, run `python test_exploit.py`
